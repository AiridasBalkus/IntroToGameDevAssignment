Character Profiles

Meet the cast of characters shaping the narrative of "Dark Streets: The Last Clue."

Protagonist

Detective: White, Stocky, Mid 40’s, John. Solves the murder haunting the town. Although unclear at first, we learn John has a strong sense of justice and always liked the town of Enniscorthy and wants it to return to the peaceful town he once knew.

Secondary

Anthony the Rookie: A stoic young man in his 20s, with a clean-shaven face, short tidy hair and a wiry frame. Helps the protagonist against the corruption in the town as he wants to help other people and do right in the world.

Helper

The Bartender Kevin: A big scary looking man in his 30’s with tattoos on both his arms and a bald head with a thick black beard. Gives clues when visited as he never liked the Gardaí and now since they face corruption, he has more of an incentive to bring them down.

Antagonists

The Higher-ups: The higher ups within the Gardaí face corruption after a single murder by them turns into a power trip. They are either motivated by the money that Gary is giving them, genuine evil, or fear of being the next victim.

Main Antagonist

Gary – The Head Gardaí: Main killer and the Boss. A tall and heavy man in his late 50’s with a greying goatee, baggy green eyes, and a wrinkly forehead. Showing signs of balding. After accidentally murdering someone in a car chase gone wrong, he wants every witness dead to protect his position at the top.

Victims of Interest

Protester/Activist: Linda McCarthy.

Journalists: Chris “Chrissy” Cunningham and Paul Jr.
